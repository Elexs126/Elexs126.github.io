"I view the humanoid body not merely as a mechanical assembly, but as the only viable vessel for a cognitive model to truly interact with and understand the physical laws of our world."

My research seeks to bridge the gap between high-level reasoning and low-level torque control. I am deeply interested in how we can instill biological plausibility and adaptive intelligence into robotic systems. Currently, my focus is on building a solid mathematical foundation and robust engineering intuition through rigorous paper reproductions and algorithmic implementations.

我的研究旨在弥合"高层认知推理"与"底层力矩控制"之间的鸿沟。我深度关注如何赋予机器人系统以生物合理性和自适应智能。目前，我通过严格的论文复现与算法实现来夯实数学基础与工程直觉。
