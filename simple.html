你好。我目前的身份是一名本科生。我对探索可以在真实物理世界中感知并活动的智慧生命的认知模型有着极大的热情。在这个愿景下，人形机器人是我用以承载并测试这种智能的躯壳。

在我的研究哲学中，我倾向于寻找那些真正能让事情 "Work"、并能引发架构范式转变的理论突破（正如 Transformer、PPO、ResNet 等所展示的力量）。我不热衷于追求脱离物理直觉的纯数学推导证明，也无意于底层硬件的繁琐落地与实体拼接调试。我执着于提出前沿理论架构的 Idea，并证明它们在系统层面是优雅且有效的。

我将全部精力投入到了对前沿科研动态的追踪、学术嗅觉的培养以及高阶课程（如 RL、凸优化等）的扎实学习中，以取代传统的绩点刷榜模式。未来，我期望能进入高校或大厂的前沿实验型研究室，作为一名理论架构导向的研究者继续深造。

Hello. I am currently an undergraduate student with a profound interest in the cognitive models of generalized intelligence that can perceive and act intuitively in the complex physical world. Under this vision, humanoid robots serve as my preferred embodiment to host and manifest this evolving cognition.

My research philosophy is driven by theoretical and architectural breakthroughs that truly 'work' and shift paradigms (akin to the impacts of Transformer, PPO, and Residual Networks). I am not inclined to pursue pure mathematical proofs devoid of physical intuition, nor do I focus on low-level hardware debugging and system deployment. I am passionate about proposing frontier architectural ideas and verifying their structural validity within the agent.

I dedicate my focus entirely to developing a sharp intuition for frontier research, tracking the rapid dynamics of the field, and digesting advanced coursework, rather than optimizing for traditional metrics. My ultimate goal is to continue as an architecture-oriented researcher in academia or a leading experimental lab.
